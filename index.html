Experience Culinary Excellence

Welcome to Greenyard Restaurant, where we serve the finest cuisine in the heart of Gaborone. Our passionate chefs create memorable dining experiences with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and innovative cooking techniques that celebrate both traditional and contemporary flavors.

Our Signature Dishes

Botswana Beef Tenderloin

Premium locally-sourced beef tenderloin, grilled to perfection and served with seasonal vegetables and our signature herb butter sauce.

Kalahari Seafood Platter

Fresh catch of the day featuring prawns, calamari, and fish fillet, prepared with aromatic spices and served with rice pilaf.

Garden Fresh Salad Bowl

Crisp mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, avocado, and seasonal fruits with our house-made vinaigrette and toasted seeds.

Traditional Setswana Platter

Authentic local cuisine featuring seswaa, pap, morogo, and traditional sides that celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Botswana.

Gourmet Pasta Selection

Handcrafted pasta with your choice of creamy mushroom, spicy arrabbiata, or classic carbonara sauce, topped with fresh herbs and parmesan.

Decadent Dessert Trio

Indulge in our chef's selection of three exquisite desserts: chocolate lava cake, vanilla panna cotta, and seasonal fruit tart.
